NovaEdit Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the planned development direction of NovaEdit.

Roadmap items may change as development progresses. Features may be added, removed, redesigned, or delayed depending on development requirements and community feedback.

Development Philosophy

NovaEdit focuses on:

* Performance
* Privacy
* Cross-platform support
* Ease of use
* Developer productivity
* Customization
* Community involvement

The goal is to create a powerful editor without unnecessary complexity.
