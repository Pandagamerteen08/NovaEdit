Overview

Next Step Studios respects user privacy.

NovaEdit is designed with privacy as a priority.

The core editor does not require:

* An account
* Personal information
* Online connection

Users can use NovaEdit locally without creating an account.

⸻

Information We Collect

By default, NovaEdit collects:

* Nothing required for normal use

Optional information may be collected if users enable certain features.

⸻

User Files

NovaEdit does not upload user files.

Projects, code, and documents remain on the user’s device.

NovaEdit only accesses files required for features selected by the user.

⸻

AI Privacy

Nova AI is designed to operate locally.

By default:

* Project files remain local.
* Conversations remain local.
* No account is required.

Users may optionally connect external AI APIs.

If an external AI provider is used, that provider’s privacy policy may apply.

⸻

AI Conversation Storage

Users may choose whether AI conversations are stored.

Storage options may include:

* Save conversations
* Delete conversations automatically
* Manage stored conversations through settings

AI history may be organized by project.

⸻

Telemetry

Telemetry is disabled by default.

Users may enable anonymous telemetry through settings.

Optional telemetry may include:

* Operating system information
* Hardware information
* Performance information
* Anonymous feature usage

Telemetry does not include:

* Source code
* Personal documents
* Project files

⸻

Crash Reports

Crash reports may be collected to improve reliability.

Users may control crash reporting settings.

Crash reports may include technical information such as:

* Error messages
* Application version
* System information

⸻

Updates

NovaEdit may check for updates.

Update checks may only determine:

* Current version
* Whether a newer version exists

Update checking frequency may be configurable.

⸻

Website Privacy

The NovaEdit website may use:

* Essential cookies
* Optional analytics cookies

Essential cookies are enabled by default.

Optional cookies can be disabled.

Users may manage cookie preferences through website settings.

⸻

Cookies

Cookie categories may include:

Essential Cookies

Used for basic website functionality.

Analytics Cookies

Used to understand website usage.

Disabled by default unless enabled by the user.

Optional Cookies

Additional functionality cookies.

Users control these preferences.

⸻

Third-Party Services

NovaEdit may use third-party services such as:

* GitHub
* Distribution platforms
* Payment providers
* AI providers chosen by users

Each service may have its own privacy policy.

⸻

Data Storage

NovaEdit primarily stores information locally.

Examples:

* Settings
* Preferences
* Project information
* AI history (if enabled)

Users control their local data.

⸻

Data Deletion

If future account systems are introduced, users may have options to request deletion of:

* Account information
* Stored preferences
* Cloud data

Availability depends on supported services.

⸻

Children’s Privacy

NovaEdit is available to users of all ages.

NovaEdit does not intentionally collect personal information from children.

Parents or guardians may contact Next Step Studios regarding privacy concerns.

⸻

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as NovaEdit grows.

Changes will be documented appropriately.
